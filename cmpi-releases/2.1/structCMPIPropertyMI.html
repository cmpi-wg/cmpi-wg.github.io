Detailed Description

CMPIPropertyMI object.

This object is provided by a property MI through MI factory functions, in order to make its MI functions available to the MB.

Addition:
Added in CMPI 2.0.
Deprecated:
Property MIs are deprecated since CMPI 2.1, in accord with the deprecation of property client operations in DMTF specifications.
